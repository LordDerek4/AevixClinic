# # Medical clinic booking app

_Started 2026-07-15 08:58 UTC_

---

## User

Design a clean, trustworthy web app for small medical clinics to manage 
patient appointment bookings and reduce no-shows.

Context:
- Users are two groups: (1) clinic staff/admin managing a schedule, and 
  (2) patients booking an appointment
- The app needs to feel calm, professional, and easy to trust — this is 
  healthcare, not a consumer app, so avoid anything flashy or overly 
  playful
- Target clinics are small practices (1-5 staff) who currently book by 
  phone or paper diary

Design the following screens/flows:
1. Patient-facing booking flow: pick a service/practitioner, see 
   available time slots, enter basic contact details, confirm booking
2. Booking confirmation screen with a summary and what happens next 
   (reminder will be sent)
3. Clinic admin dashboard: calendar/list view of today's and upcoming 
   appointments, ability to see patient contact info, mark 
   appointments as completed/no-show
4. Simple staff login screen
5. A settings screen where clinic staff can set their available hours 
   and reminder timing (e.g. send reminder 24 hours before)

Style direction:
- Clean, modern, minimal — plenty of white space
- A calming color palette (soft blues/greens are common in health 
  contexts, but choose something that doesn't feel generic)
- Clear typography that's easy to read for all ages, since patients 
  booking may not be tech-savvy
- Mobile-first for the patient booking flow, since most patients will 
  book from their phone

Output the design as a working HTML/CSS mockup (or React components) 
I can review before we build out the actual functionality.
